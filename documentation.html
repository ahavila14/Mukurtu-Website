Q: What is Mukurtu?

A: Mukurtu is a free and open source community archive platform designed with the unique needs of Indigenous communities, libraries, archives, and museums in mind. Mukurtu is built on the Drupal content management system with a features set aimed at Indigenous cultural heritage management needs.

Back to top

Q: What are the server requirements?

A: Mukurtu is an installation profile for Drupal 7, so your server or host needs to meet Drupal 7 requirements. We're linking to the Drupal 7 core requirements page, which is maintained by the awesome Drupal community. See our wiki if you get stuck.

Back to top

Q: How do I install Mukurtu?

A: Installing Mukurtu is as easy as installing Drupal. This means you’ll need to follow the Drupal install guide, and be familiar with setting up a web server. We have great instructions for you on our wiki.

Back to top

Q: Who uses Mukurtu?

A: Mukurtu was established to provide Indigenous groups with a simple-to-use, powerful, content management system with fine grain control over their own digital cultural heritage. Join a growing community of users who are looking for alternatives that better serve Indigenous needs.

Back to top

Q: Why Drupal?

A: Mukurtu started as a grassroots effort in 2005 and has developed into a robust, mature platform. Drupal provides a large, thriving development community with a proven track record in sustainability and responsiveness. We have big plans for Mukurtu and the Drupal community is helping to make our dreams possible.

Back to top

Q: Can each site have a custom domain?

A: Yes. You are free to install Mukurtu on any server you wish, and rebrand the archive as you see fit, so long as you adhere to our licensing terms.

Free and Open Source

Mukurtu is provided free of charge to download, customize and adapt for your digital archive and content management needs. Mukurtu is also an open source software tool suite licensed using a GPL. Follow our development blog and join a user community to create more plugins or get answers to any of your questions.

Q: This still sounds hard, is there an easier option for installing and running Murkutu?

A: Yes. We are working with our development partners to produce a one-click install hosted solution that will be super easy to configure and manage. Stay tuned!

Q: Who owns the content?

A: You are solely responsible for the content in your Mukurtu archive, which is why the software was designed as an open source, downloadable solution. You run it, you control it, and you manage all of the content within it.

Back to top

Q: What are extensions, plugins and themes?

A: Mukurtu comes pre-installed with several themes so you can tailor the site to your liking. Because we’ve built Mukurtu on Drupal, a lot of powerful functionality comes right out of the box, painlessly and professionally configured by our development team. Many of the plugins and extensions, such as geo-tagging images, can be turned off or customized.
